My name is Justin Shum, and I am a professional ball polisher. I began my interest in the career at a very young age. My family has a long line of adamant ball polishers. After my first experience of ball polishing with my dad on a hot summer day, it has fueled my passion in life since. Since then, I followed my father for advice and studied until I was accepted to Ball Cleaner University of Michigan, BCU During my time at BCU, I was a leading contributor to the research and development of Spherical Aerodynamics club. In particular, our research into the most efficient velocity of air to perfectly clean a rubber ball has been awarded at a recent competition. After 4 years of dedication, I graduated with a 3.7 GPA in 2021.

After my graduation, I was offered a position with Dyson on their research and development team in developing new and more efficient ways to clean balls.My time there consisted from 2014 to 2023. My time there was cut short due to a conflict of interest in ideologies. While they were reaching for a more affordable method to achieving perfection, I argued that there is no way to shortcut the process. To achieve optimal results, we would need optimal funding. I did not back down from my principles, so I decided to take my talents elsewhere. After a few years taking odd jobs around where I live, I have gathered a large set of experience everywhere adjacent to ball polishing.

My experience in my work is something I take pride in. I have learned every aspect of the process from the bottom level to the most senior. I know my work, and I will never take a step back in quality for anything less than perfect. Serious offers only.
